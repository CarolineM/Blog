About Me

Name: Adriana
Occupation: ESL teacher.
Interests: Healthful living, how to make a priority list, human relationships, food, self development, the funny side of life.
Hobbies: Writing.

Hi! My name is Adriana, and this is my Website. This site exists in order to showcase my writing on several different topics, in hopes of one day making a partial living as a journalist and/or novelist, or some other facet of writing, and also to share some articles and lessons I have created for multi-level learners of English as a Second Language, my specialty field. If you are a motivated language learner, I sincerely believe that I have a wealth of tips, advice and methods for you to improve your English and begin speaking and understanding at a much more sophisticated level. I believe in my own skills as an English teacher and language learner, (I study Spanish at the pre-intermediate level), and feel that I have a lot more to offer students than what can be taught or learned in a traditional classroom.

Thank you for visiting my Website, Adriana English!

If you have any questions or comments, please email me at adrijdin@gmail.com.
